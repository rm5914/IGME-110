About the Author

J. K. Rowling born Joanne Rowling, was born on July 31, 1965 in Yate, Gloucestershire, England. She is a British author, film producer, television producer, screenwriter, and philantropists known for famous book series Harry Potter. She got the idea of creating the Harry Potter series back in 1990 when she was sitting on a delayed train ride from Manchester to London. Eventually, her first book came out in 1996 called Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone renamed to Sorcerer's Stone for readers in America.
